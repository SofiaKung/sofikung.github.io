Good KPIs tell you what to do next. In this post, I break down a pragmatic approach to selecting metrics that drive decisions instead of dashboards that gather dust.

Principles

  • KPIs must map to an action owner and cadence.
  • Prefer rate or ratio metrics over raw counts.
  • Instrument counter-metrics to catch unintended consequences.

Templates

For each KPI: definition, owner, target, review cadence, and playbook.
